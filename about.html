About High Point Fabrics

Weaving a Legacy of American Quality

Our Story

Founded in the heart of America's textile belt, High Point Fabrics, Inc. has grown from a small, dedicated mill into a leading manufacturer of premium fabrics. Our journey is one of passion for the craft, an unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep respect for our heritage.

We believe in the power of American manufacturing. Every thread we weave is a testament to the skill of our team and our dedication to producing textiles that are both beautiful and durable, designed to inspire and built to last.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple: combine innovation with integrity. We continuously invest in technology to push the boundaries of textile design while upholding the ethical and sustainable practices that define us.

Uncompromising Quality

Collaborative Partnership

We work closely with our clients to achieve their vision.

Protecting our planet for generations to come.
